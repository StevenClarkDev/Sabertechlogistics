Saber Tech Launches Cutting-Edge Platform to Transform Last-Mile Delivery

Saber Tech today announced the beta launch of its Last-Mile Courier Platform, a production-ready system the company operates to power real-world delivery execution and visibility. This milestone marks Saber Tech's first fully deployable platform, now running live across the United States and Canada.

Translate »

The beta focuses exclusively on last-mile courier operations, delivering reliable dispatch coordination, real-time tracking, exception handling, and operational visibility across local and regional networks. It is purpose-built to ensure on-time, transparent, and professional delivery execution, without attempting to serve as a full supply chain or transportation management system.

By directly operating the platform, Saber Tech maintains complete control over performance, reliability, and day-to-day operations, allowing the team to refine and optimize the system in real-world conditions.

While the company envisions a broader logistics ecosystem, this beta prioritizes stability and consistent last-mile performance, laying the foundation for scalable, dependable delivery solutions.

With this beta launch, Saber Tech solidifies its reputation as an operator-first technology company, deploying systems only when they are proven ready for real-world operations. Each release is deliberately scoped to ensure fully deployable, production-ready performance, with additional platform functionality introduced only when it meets the same rigorous operational standards.
