Why ATMP Uses Progressive Web Apps

ATMP leverages Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) to ensure consistent business logic across all devices. Updates deploy instantly, no app store delays, so owners and operators maintain full control over live operations.

PWAs deliver real-time push notifications, offline resilience, and secure access while reducing maintenance overhead. This approach minimizes downtime and operational friction, supporting efficiency and profitability for every delivery.
