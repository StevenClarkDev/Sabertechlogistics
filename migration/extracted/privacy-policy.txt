Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1/29/26

Saber Tech (“Saber Tech,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the ATMP-Core and ATMP-Courier platforms, including all related websites, applications, and services (collectively, the “Platform”). We are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of all users who interact with our Platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you access or use our services as a customer, driver, business partner, or visitor.

 
1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate a secure, efficient, and enforcement-driven logistics platform.

Personal Information

When you register or use the Platform, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, login credentials, and profile details. For drivers, we may also collect government-issued identification, driver’s license details, vehicle information, insurance documents, and verification records.

Financial Information

To process payments and protect revenue integrity, we may collect billing details, payment method information, transaction history, and payout records. Payment data is processed securely through authorized third-party payment providers.

Operational & Usage Information

We automatically collect technical and operational data, including IP address, browser type, device information, timestamps, acceptance windows, availability status, dispatch activity, and delivery performance metrics.

Location Information

Location data may be collected in real time to enable dispatch coordination, route optimization, availability enforcement, and delivery confirmation.

 
2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to operate and improve the Platform, including but not limited to managing dispatch workflows, validating driver availability, enforcing acceptance windows, processing payments, preventing fraud, and maintaining system accountability.

Your information helps us ensure operational accuracy, system reliability, regulatory compliance, and secure execution of deliveries from order creation through completion.

 
3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Saber Tech does not sell or rent personal data.

We may share information with trusted third parties such as payment processors, identity verification providers, cloud infrastructure partners, and analytics services solely for operational purposes. We may also disclose information when required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or security of the Platform and its users.

 
4. Data Retention

We retain personal and operational data only for as long as necessary to fulfill business, legal, and regulatory requirements. Upon account termination, data may be retained for compliance, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, or audit purposes and may later be anonymized for analytical use.

 
5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards, including encryption, access controls, authentication systems, and continuous monitoring. While we strive to protect all data, users acknowledge that no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

 
6. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information, subject to applicable laws and operational requirements. Requests can be submitted through official support channels and will be reviewed in accordance with legal obligations.

 
7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
